Welcome to the Plant Diseases Guide

Plants are vital, but they face various diseases. Learn how to protect them in style!

Common Plant Diseases

Powdery Mildew

A fungal disease causing white, powdery spots on plant leaves.

Leaf Spot

Bacterial or fungal infection causing spots on leaves.

Prevention Tips

  • Water plants early in the morning to prevent fungal growth.
  • Use resistant plant varieties for common diseases.
  • Clean up fallen leaves and debris regularly.
